MY DIMENSION FRONT OF HOUSE

KAPPA FUTURFESTIVAL

In the context of one of the beating hearts of electronic music on a global scale, I take part in the organizational machinery that makes the festival possible. Among the industrial architectures of Parco Dora and the energy of an international audience, I handle access management and shipments during the setup, event, and dismantling phases, as well as coordinating staff accreditation. My role fits into the delicate logistical balance that supports the event, contributing to the transformation of urban space into a large shared stage of music, people, and movement.

MILANO SKATING ARENA

In the context of the preparations for the Milano–Cortina 2026 Winter Olympics, I took part in logistical operations at the Milano Skating Arena. As Venue Logistic Supplier Manager, I coordinated workforce and vehicles during the venue setup phase, contributing to the management of logistical flows and the coordination between the different suppliers involved in creating the space designated for the Olympic competitions.

OLYMPIC & PARALYMPIC VILLAGE MILANO

Within the framework of the Milano–Cortina 2026 Winter Olympics and Paralympic Games, I also took part in the logistical operations of the Olympic & Paralympic Village of Milan. As Venue Logistic Supplier Manager, I coordinated workforce and material-handling vehicles within the venue during the setup, event, and dismantling phases of the village, as well as overseeing the bump-in and bump-out operations of the installations. In this context, the work contributed to the creation of a space dedicated to athletes, the true heart of the Olympic experience.

FISU WORLD UNIVERSITY GAMES

In the context of the FISU World University Games, one of the largest multidisciplinary sporting events dedicated to university athletes from all over the world, I served as Education Projects Coordinator. Within an event that brings together sport, culture, and international exchange, I contributed to the development and coordination of educational projects designed to enrich the experience of athletes and delegations beyond the sporting competition. My work focused on the planning and management of educational activities, collaboration with institutional partners, and operational support for educational initiatives, helping to transform the university games into a space for encounter, learning, and dialogue among young people from diverse cultural and social backgrounds.

SPECIAL OLYMPICS WORLD WINTER GAMES

During the Special Olympics World Winter Games, a major international winter sports event that brought together more than 1,500 athletes from over 100 countries competing across eight disciplines, celebrating inclusion, passion, and sportsmanship in the heart of Piedmont, between Turin and the Italian Alpine venues, I served as Venue Media & Broadcast Manager. In this role, I coordinated all activities related to crew access, media operations, and broadcast flow management within the venue, ensuring that the audiovisual coverage of the event was efficient and supported by solid logistical planning. My work facilitated collaboration between technical teams, international media, and organizational partners, enabling the smooth transmission of the most significant moments of the Games and supporting the storytelling of an event that brings together sport, humanity, and global visibility.

ABOUT ME

I work in event organization and logistics, operating in dynamic and complex environments where coordination, precision, and adaptability are essential. Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work across major international events, music festivals, and sport-related projects, contexts in which the work behind the scenes becomes what allows the final experience to take shape.

In my work, I mainly focus on logistical and operational aspects: managing flows, coordinating the workforce, and collaborating with suppliers and technical teams. I enjoy working at the intersection of organization and operations, where every detail contributes to making a larger system function smoothly.

I’m a creative, a thinker, a perfectionist; who enjoys thinking outside the box and finding new solutions to the problems that inevitably arise during the realization of an event. For me, every project is a balance between organization, people, and spaces: the goal is always to ensure that everything runs smoothly, even as complexity grows. And of course, having fun during the process.
